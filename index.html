Our Story

Founded in 2020, FreshSqueeze began with a simple mission: to bring the freshest, most nutritious juices to health-conscious consumers. Our founders, Sarah and Michael, were passionate about creating products that not only taste incredible but also fuel your body with natural goodness.

We source our ingredients from local organic farms, ensuring each bottle is packed with vitamins, minerals, and enzymes. Our cold-press process preserves nutrients and flavor, delivering a superior product that stands out from shelf-stable alternatives.
